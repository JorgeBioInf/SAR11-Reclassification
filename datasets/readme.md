Examples of **intermediate files** and **obtained results** are here stored.

This project started with the dataset provided by [Free et al (2024)](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2024.12.24.630191v3) : [Original dataset].(https://figshare.com/articles/dataset/Supplementary_tables_for_SAR11_genomes_from_the_tropical_Pacific_study_Freel_et_al_2024_/28087490/1?file=51364793)

The table `supplementary_data` was also retrieved from [Free et al (2024)](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2024.12.24.630191v3)


Final **genome-wise classification** results can be found in `Genomes_classification.tsv`.

Final **clade-wise classification** results are summarized in `Classification_byclade_uniques.tsv`.
